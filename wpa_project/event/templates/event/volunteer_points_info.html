Volunteer Points

Volunteer points are earned by volunteering for our classes as well as other events. Volunteer points can be used to pay for classes or for reimbursement of approved purchases, each point is worth $1 for such purchases. Instructor certification training may be reimbursed at $3 per point. See the Volunteer Incentive Policy for more information.
