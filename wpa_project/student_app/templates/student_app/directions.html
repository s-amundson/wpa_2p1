Directions

From the Southwest:

Take the 101 east and exit at the Balboa off ramp. Turn left at the bottom of the ramp and go north to Burbank. Turn right and go east to Woodley. Turn left and go north until you see the signs for the water treatment plant, the Wildlife Refuge and the Japanese Gardens on the right. Turn right into the park and stay to the right at the fork. Follow the road all the way, past the park and the cricket fields, until you reach the traffic circle. Go left and up into the range parking lot.

From the Southeast:

Take the 101 west and exit at the Balboa off ramp. Turn right at the bottom of the ramp and go north to Burbank. Turn right and go east to Woodley. Turn left and go north until you see the signs for the water treatment plant, the Wildlife Refuge and the Japanese Gardens on the right. Turn right into the park and stay to the right at the fork. Follow the road all the way, past the park and the cricket fields, until you reach the traffic circle. Go left and up into the range parking lot.

From the Northeast:

Take the 405 south and exit at the Burbank off ramp. Turn right on Burbank and go west to Woodley. Turn right on Woodley Ave and go North, take the first entrance to the right. Follow the road though the parking lot then take a right. Take a left at the traffic circle to the range parking lot.

From the Northeast:

Take the 101 west and exit at the Balboa off ramp. Turn right at the bottom of the ramp and go north to Burbank. Turn right and go east to Woodley. Turn left and go north until you see the signs for the water treatment plant, the Wildlife Refuge and the Japanese Gardens on the right. Turn right into the park and stay to the right at the fork. Follow the road all the way, past the park and the cricket fields, until you reach the traffic circle. Go left and up into the range parking lot.

From the Northwest:

Take the 118 east to the 405 south. Take the 405 south and exit at the Burbank off ramp. Turn right on Burbank and go west to Woodley. Turn right on Woodley Ave and go North, take the first entrance to the right. Follow the road though the parking lot then take a right. Take a left at the traffic circle to the range parking lot.

From the North:

Take the 5 south to the 405 south. Take the 405 south and exit at the Burbank off ramp. Turn right on Burbank and go west to Woodley. Turn right on Woodley Ave and go North, take the first entrance to the right. Follow the road though the parking lot then take a right. Take a left at the traffic circle to the range parking lot.

From the South:

Take the 405 north and exit at the Burbank off ramp. Turn left on Burbank and go west to Woodley. Turn right on Burbank and go west to Woodley. Turn right on Woodley Ave and go North, take the first entrance to the right. Follow the road though the parking lot then take a right. Take a left at the traffic circle to the range parking lot.

Turn right and go east to Woodley. Turn left and go north take the first entrance to the right. Follow the road though the parking lot then take a right.
