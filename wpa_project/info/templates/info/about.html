About the Woodley Park Archers

Welcome to the Home of the Woodley Park Archers.

Woodley Park Archers promotes fellowship through social and field activities offered by the organization. WPA is a non-sectarian, non-political, educational organization.

Membership in Woodley Park Archers includes Compound, Olympic-Style Recurve and Traditional style archers, archery enthusiasts, family members and others who support the club’s objectives and, as members, may participate in activities, meetings and classes.

Woodley Park Archers is a Member Club of USA Archery and the State Archers of California.

Woodley Park Archery Range is a public facility maintained by the Woodley Park Archers, a 501(c)(3) non-profit, all volunteer organization, operating under permit from the City of Los Angeles Department of Recreation and Parks(RAP).

The Woodley Park Archers oversees, manages, and regulates activities and operates Instructional programming at the range with low cost classes in which the community can learn and enjoy the art of archery. Class registration fees and donations from students and users of the range help to support the classes and maintenance of the equipment and facility. All losses and destruction at the range are absorbed by the donations from members of the public and not from RAP support or taxpayer funding. Tax deductible donations can be made to Woodley Park Archers by clicking here.

Mission Statement

The objective of the Woodley Park Archers is the elevation of the sport of archery through education and the promotion of youth, family and individual archery participation.

The Woodley Park Archers promotes the safe use of archery equipment, the practice of proper archery techniques, etiquette and sportsmanship.

Coaching

The Woodley Park Archers boasts an excellent coaching staff of volunteer instructors who can offer assistance in all forms of archery – Traditional, Olympic-style and Compound. All the club’s instructors are certified by USA Archery (USAA), the governing body for target archery in the United States. Click here for a list of our coaches and staff.
